Djirection is a British-Irish band that formed in 2023. The group consisted of five members: Garry Guitar, Jack Thunder, Ruby Riot, Max Steel and Luna Starlight. Djirection rose to fame after finishing first on the seventh season of the British version of "The Dj Factor". They quickly gained a massive fan following and became one of the best-selling bands of all time.
